LG Wonderbox Showcase 2022

ToolsCinema4D, Houdini, Octane, Forester, X-Particles
AwardTop 3 Finalist
VenueLG Times Square Billboard, NYC

I participated in a digital art contest called LG WonderBox Showcase and was lucky enough to be nominated as a top 3 finalist!

Made with Cinema4D, particles from Houdini, rendered in Octane (Forester and X-Particles plugins used). Houdini particle text morph using Vellum grain.

Never expected my work to be on a giant Times Square screen — it was amazing!
