Student Thesis Film · 2023

"Cycle"

Somewhere in the vast universe, a supernova explosion occurs. An asteroid from the explosion lands on a nearby planet, triggering new life on it.

Inspired by the idea that we are all children of the stars — life cycles through the universe. Created entirely using Houdini, Redshift, and Nuke.
